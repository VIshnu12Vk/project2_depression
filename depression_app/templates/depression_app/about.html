About us

Our depression testing tool is designed to provide you with an initial assessment of your depressive symptoms. It consists of a series of questions that will help gauge your emotional well-being and provide an indication of whether you may be experiencing symptoms of depression.

Please keep in mind that the results of this test are not a substitute for professional diagnosis or medical advice. If you have concerns about your mental health or suspect you may be experiencing depression, we strongly encourage you to seek guidance from a qualified healthcare professional.

Our team is committed to creating a safe and supportive environment for all users. We prioritize user privacy and ensure that any personal information you provide during the testing process is handled securely and confidentially.
